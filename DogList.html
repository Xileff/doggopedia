Shiba Inu

Breed of dog that originated in Japan some 3,000 years ago for small-game and ground-bird hunting.
Golden Retriever

One of the most popular dog breeds in the United States. The breed's friendly, tolerant attitude makes them great family pets.
Siberian Husky

The Siberian is a friendly and gentle dog who is not overly suspicious of strangers or aggressive toward other dogs.
Beagle

Beagles are muscular, solid dogs with a slightly domed skull. Their muzzle is squarish and the nose broad. Ears are long and droopy.
Alaskan Malamute

This arctic breed has a thick, pale coat. The malamute is the most wolf-like dog when it comes to appearance.
Pomeranian

Pomeranians are known for being smart, curious, and energetic. They are typically very playful and love to be the center of attention.
